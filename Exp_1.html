Anirudh Ravichander - The Musical Rockstar

About Anirudh

Anirudh Ravichander is a celebrated Indian music composer and playback singer. He took the world by storm with his debut track "Why This Kolaveri Di" and has since produced blockbuster hits across Tamil, Telugu, and Hindi cinema. His energetic stage performances and genre-blending music have earned him a loyal global fanbase.
